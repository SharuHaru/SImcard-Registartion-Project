Your Guide To SIM Registration

What is SIM Registration

Republic Act No. 11934 the SIM Registration Act requires the registration of all SIMs, including those in card and electronic form, as a prerequisite to activation. Under the measure, users of all mobile devices, including prepaid broadband devices, must register their SIMs.
